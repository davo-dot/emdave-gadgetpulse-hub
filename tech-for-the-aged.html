Tech for the Aged Initiative

Bridging the digital divide for elderly community members

Our Mission

We are dedicated to helping elderly community members navigate technology safely and confidently. Through free workshops, one-on-one support, and scam-prevention education, we ensure seniors stay connected with family, shop safely online, and protect their personal information.

What We Offer

  • Digital-literacy workshops at senior centers
  • One-on-one device setup & security
  • Ongoing tech-help hotline
  • Community outreach & partnerships
  • Scam-prevention seminars

Program Benefits

  • Stay connected with family via video calls
  • Shop & bank safely online
  • Recognize phishing & fraud attempts
  • Use smartphones & tablets confidently
  • Access tele-health & online services
